Concerns & FAQ

Clear expectations. No surprises.

Before we start

  • Copyright & Assets: All licensed stock, music, and provided footage are cleared for commercial use.
  • Confidentiality: NDA friendly. Unreleased content is kept strictly secure.
  • Revision Policy: Revisions cover pacing, text, and minor graphical changes. Fundamental structural changes require a new brief.
  • Turnaround Times: Clock starts upon receipt of all required raw assets.
  • Platform Compliance: Exports are calibrated to exact platform specs (bitrate, safe zones).

Standard delivery is MP4 (H.264/H.265) in 1080p or 4K. Project files (Premiere/After Effects) are available on Pro tiers or as an add-on.

50% upfront to commence work, 50% upon final approval before un-watermarked delivery. Monthly retainers are billed on the 1st of the cycle.

We use dedicated Google Drive or Frame.io folders for seamless high-speed transfers and direct video feedback.
